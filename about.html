About Me

Capturing Life Through the Lens

Hello! I’m Jamrez, a passionate photographer based in Watamu. I specialize in turning real moments into timeless images that tell meaningful stories.

With over 7 years of experience, I’ve worked across genres—from portraits and weddings to editorial and travel. My approach is rooted in honesty and creativity, using light and emotion as my tools.

Whether I’m documenting people or landscapes, I aim to create images that feel authentic, intimate, and lasting. Photography isn’t just what I do — it’s how I see the world.
